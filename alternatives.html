Okay, so for whatever reason you can't get your hands on a book.

Maybe you're stranded waiting for someone to come fix your car, or the library just closed and you don't have the budget to buy a book, or some other unfortunte combination of circumstnces. I get it, it happens.

Here are some other ideas:

Call a friend to catch up.

Do some yoga or stretch.

Meditate.

Make a list of things for which you are grateful.

Plan for how to get a book so you're not stuck in this situation again.
