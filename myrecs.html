Also, some tips on how to make friends:

Find a group of people with similar interests. Maybe take a class. Colleges often offer community art and language classes. You could also take music or dance classes or join a community band. Gyms offer athletic classes. Churches might have Bible study groups. Charitable organizations, hospitals, political organizations, and museums may need volunteers. Pick something that interests you!

Once you find a group of people who have something in common with you, just start talking about those interests! Ask people about themselves and listen.
